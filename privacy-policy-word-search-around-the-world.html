Introduction

LACX Studios ("we," "our," or "us") built Word Search: Around the World ("the Game") as a free, ad-supported mobile application available on Android and iOS platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Game. By downloading or using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Game.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in with your Google account or Apple ID to save your game progress, we receive your user ID, display name, and email address solely for the purpose of cloud save functionality. If you use Apple's "Hide My Email" feature, we will receive a private relay email address instead of your real email. We do not access any other data from your account.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Progress Data: Your game progress, including completed levels, stars earned, souvenirs collected, stamps, and settings preferences, is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google or Apple, this data may be synced to the cloud to allow progress recovery across devices.
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partner, Google AdMob, may automatically collect certain information including your device's advertising identifier (Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS), device information (model, operating system version), IP address, and general location (city-level) to serve relevant ads. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Purchase Information: If you make an in-app purchase (such as removing advertisements), the transaction is processed entirely by the platform's app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. We only receive confirmation of whether a purchase was completed.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Phone numbers or physical addresses
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Microphone or camera data
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Messages, emails, or call logs
  • Browsing history

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Game Progress: To save and restore your game progress locally and, if you opt in, across devices via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In.
  • Advertising: To display advertisements within the Game through Google AdMob. You may watch optional rewarded ads to receive in-game benefits (such as hints).
  • Purchases: To process and verify in-app purchases and provide ad-free experience to users who purchase the ad removal option.

Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising): Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services (in-app purchases and sign-in on Android): Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store (in-app purchases on iOS): Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (optional cloud save on Android): Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In (optional cloud save on iOS): Apple Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of Google and Apple to understand how they handle your data.

Advertising

The Game displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your Advertising ID and device information to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Turn off Personalized Ads. You can also manage app tracking via Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

You may also remove all advertisements from the Game by purchasing the ad-free option available within the Game.

Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction, such as 16 in certain European countries under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable minimum age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), GDPR, and similar regulations, if we determine that a user is under the applicable minimum age, personalized advertising will be disabled for that user.

Consent for Personalized Advertising

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK, the Game will request your consent before serving personalized advertisements, in accordance with GDPR requirements. You may choose to receive only non-personalized ads. You can change your advertising preferences at any time through the Game's Settings menu or through your device settings.

On iOS, the Game will also comply with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, requesting your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local Data: Game progress stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the Game or clearing the app data through your device settings.
  • Cloud Save Data: If you used Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In to save your progress, you may request deletion of your cloud-saved data by contacting us at the email address below. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Advertising Data: Data collected by Google AdMob is retained and managed by Google in accordance with their data retention policies.

To request deletion of your data, you may:

  1. Uninstall the Game to remove all local data
  2. Contact us at the email below to request deletion of cloud-saved data
  3. Reset your Advertising ID through your device settings

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. Game progress is stored locally on your device and, if you opt in, synced via Google's or Apple's secure infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restriction: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data, including for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint if you believe your privacy has been breached. We will respond to complaints within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may escalate your complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

  • Cessation of Third-Party Provision: Request that we stop providing your personal information to third parties.
